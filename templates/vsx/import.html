About VSX

The AAVSO International Variable Star Index (VSX) is a comprehensive database of variable stars maintained by the American Association of Variable Star Observers. It contains data on hundreds of thousands of variable stars including their types, periods, magnitude ranges, and coordinates.

Variable Star Types
  • CEP - Cepheid variables
  • M - Mira long-period variables
  • EA/EB/EW - Eclipsing binaries
  • RR - RR Lyrae variables
  • SR - Semi-regular variables
  • DSCT - Delta Scuti variables
  • UG - Dwarf novae
